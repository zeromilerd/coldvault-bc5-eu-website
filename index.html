ColdVault HS with Hard Switch is also a tiny form factor removable USB ported memory device with a hardware switch built into the device itself. It currently remains a concept because the MUDP chips currently on the market do not provide On/Off connector as traditional USB devices do not need it.


Commercial mass production of redesigned MUDP chips with ColdVault connector require large quantitates, which is not possible at prototyping stage. Nevertheless, we did produce a 3D printed ColdVault HS device with physical hardware switch ready for operations when the modified MUDP chips are made available.

ColdVault Chipset is a complete kit for computer manufacturers for integrating ColdVault into their existing devices without any major alterations to the motherboard or the housing.

The ColdVault chipset has 3 components, the MUDP chip provides the flash storage for the data, the female connector hosts the MUDP chip and is soldered to the motherboard to draw power and exchange data, and the switch flushes with the computer housing for user's easy access while it controls the MUDP power supply and data transmission as an interrupt between the the female connectors power / data pins and the mother board.
